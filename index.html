Rizal Day
Rizal Day

Dr. Jose Rizal, a national hero of the Philippines, was executed by firing squad at Bagumbayan (now Rizal Park) in Manila, a pivotal moment in the Philippine Revolution. His execution, ordered by the Spanish colonial government, solidified his status as a martyr and inspired the Filipino people to fight for independence. The anniversary of his death is now commemorated as Rizal Day, a national holiday in the Philippines.
